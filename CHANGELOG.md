## [1.0.0] - 2024-XX-XX

### Added
- 🍔 **Oversaturation System** — New food track that activates when hunger is full
- 🛡️ **Fat Armor** — Additional hearts that absorb damage
- 🦘 **Slime Bounce** — Bounce off blocks like a slime (10+ stacks)
- 💥 **Shockwave** — AoE damage on landing from heights (25+ stacks)
- 📏 **Character Growth** — Player grows physically with oversaturation
- ⚖️ **Scales Block** — Redstone-compatible weight measurement block
- 🎨 **Custom HUD** — Burger counter, fat hearts, stack counter, stats panel
- 🍽️ **Food Variety System** — Tracks recent foods and blocks repeats
- ⚔️ **Combat Bonuses** — Attack damage, knockback resistance, melee repulse
- 📏 **Reach Bonuses** — Increased attack distance and block reach
- 🔧 **Full Configuration** — 50+ configurable options
- 🔊 **Sound Effects** — Scales weighing and shockwave sounds
- 🏷️ **Mod Compatibility** — Farmer's Delight, Vanilla Delight, Wizardry Delight

### Technical
- Server-side config sync to client
- Keybinding system for ability toggles
- Mixin-based integration with vanilla and modded blocks
- Attachment-based player data persistence
- Redstone signal output from Scales block
