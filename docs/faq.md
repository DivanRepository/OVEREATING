# ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is Overeating Plus?
Overeating Plus is a Minecraft mod that adds an **oversaturation system** — a new food track that activates when your hunger bar is full. Eat more to gain powerful bonuses and unlock unique abilities!

### What Minecraft versions are supported?
Currently, Overeating Plus supports **Minecraft 1.21.1** with **NeoForge 21.1.230+**.

### Is this mod server-side only?
No, Overeating Plus is required on both **client and server** for proper functionality.

---

## Gameplay Questions

### How do I start gaining oversaturation?
1. Fill your hunger bar completely (20/20)
2. Make sure your saturation is above the threshold (default: 5.0)
3. Keep eating! You'll start gaining oversaturation points

### Why can't I eat certain foods?
The **Food Variety System** prevents you from eating the same food repeatedly when in oversaturation mode. Try eating different foods!

### How do I toggle abilities?
- **Slime Bounce:** Press `G`
- **Shockwave:** Press `H`

### Why am I getting slower?
Movement slowdown is a penalty that starts at 1 oversaturation stack. This is balanced by the other bonuses you receive.

### What happens when I die?
All oversaturation progress is reset on death.

## Configuration Questions

### Where is the config file?
The config file is located at `config/overatingplus-server.toml`.

### Can I disable certain features?
Yes! Almost every feature can be configured or disabled in the config file.

### Can I change the keybinds?
Currently, keybinds are hardcoded to `G` and `H`. This may be changed in future updates.
